Madden (Electronic Arts)

Entertainment / Computer Games

TL/DR: Tell a compelling story - forget about the medium and the time that you have to tell it. A good story can be cut-down or extended. A bad story is always just a bad story - no matter how you tell it.

Best Bit: The impact of the HERO (5min 'movie trailer') - is extended and made even better with a HUB of additional content (a 'season of episodes') using puppets! 

Steal This: Multiple versions of the same scene - personalised to the viewer's team. Each scene relevant to what's actually going on in 'real life' (current affairs + personalisation = 'right-now-relevance').

Key Behavioural Triggers: FUN, PERSONALISATION, SOCIETY.

HK Express

Travel

TL/DR: Earn your holidays by doing what you do - the longer you stay at work - the more discount you get to use on your travel package.

Best Bit: Make the overtime hours that people work more meaningful than just 'overtime pay'.

Steal This: Instead of shaping the behavior towards the reward - create the reward around the current behaviour

VOLVO AUTOMOTIVE

TL/DR: Volvo - a Swedish (or more accurately, Chinese) automotive manufacturer 'intercepts' US car brands during their biggest advertising day of the year - by running a social media promotion that invites viewers to 'win a car by tweeting to Volvo whenever they see ANY car commercial during the Superbowl.

Best Bit: They didn't even run an TV spot.

Steal This: Use our competitors ad spend money to help run our own campaigns.
